A Special Genesis

To initiate the admission process, you need to submit the following documents at our main school.
  1. 1. Copy of Birth Certificate and Passport
  2. 2. Copy of School Reports from the Previous School

Upon the submission of the above documents, the school can then organize placement testing for the grade level the applicant is applying for.

School tour

Families are encouraged to visit the school for a tour of the campus and talk with our admission staff. Appointments can be made by contacting the school administration.

Admission Decisions

Admission decisions are made after careful evaluation of the candidates. Results of diagnostic testing and previous school records are considered on a case-by-case basis. Additional criteria for admission include motivation, social and emotional development, willingness to work hard, and the ability of Tender Care Junior Academy to accommodate the needs of the candidate. Once accepted and the required fees paid, a place for the student is reserved.
