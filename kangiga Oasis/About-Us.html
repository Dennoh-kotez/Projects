Our Story

Kanginga School's success story is a testament to the unwavering dedication of its visionary leadership, committed educators, and enthusiastic students. From its humble beginnings, the school embarked on a journey of academic excellence and holistic development. Under the dynamic leadership of Principal Sarah Njoroge, the school implemented innovative teaching methodologies and embraced cutting-edge technologies to enhance the learning experience. The dedicated team of educators tirelessly worked towards nurturing a conducive learning environment, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and instilling a sense of responsibility in each student

Our Approach

Kanginga Oasis Schools fraternity distinguishes itself through a comprehensive and forward-thinking approach to education that prioritizes the holistic development of its students. Embracing a student-centered philosophy, the school focuses on fostering not only academic excellence but also the development of essential life skills, building confidence at an early age. The approach is rooted in innovative teaching methodologies, integrating technology, project-based learning, and collaborative activities to enhance the overall educational experience. Our faculty members go beyond traditional teaching methods, emphasizing critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity to prepare students for the challenges of the future

Events

Kangiga School takes immense pride in consistently achieving excellent marks in not only the KCPE (Kenya Certificate of Primary Education) exams but also involving students with various sports activities.. These outstanding results and activities respectively are a testament to the unwavering commitment of our dedicated educators,coaches and the hard work of our diligent students
