Welcome to Kanginga Oasis Schools

Our School:

Kangiga Oasis School is a vibrant educational institution nestled in a picturesque environment, offering a nurturing and inspiring learning experience. With a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, the school provides a welcoming atmosphere for students to thrive intellectually, socially, and emotionally. The lush surroundings create a tranquil backdrop for a comprehensive educational journey, where dedicated educators and staff work collaboratively to cultivate a sense of curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity in every student. Kangiga Oasis School is not just an educational institution; it's a community that fosters a love for learning and prepares students to navigate the challenges of the future with confidence and a well-rounded skill set.

Our Primary School Motto:

"Discipline and hardwork Beget Success."

Mission:

"Promoting academic, spiritual & extra curricular skills necessary to develop a holistic person ready to face life challenges with diligence & integrity in a competitive world."

Vision:

"To be the best school in Kenya in adequately preparing students to face future challenges"
