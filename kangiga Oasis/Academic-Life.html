High School

The Girls High School, encompassing Forms 1 - 4, stands as a beacon of academic excellence and personal growth. These years serve as a sensitive training towards both academical and social orientation towards real life competence.

Junior Secondary

This group comprises of Grade 7 up to Grade 9. These pivotal years serve as a bridge between the foundational knowledge acquired in earlier grades and the advanced concepts introduced in high school.

Primary School

Our academic guidance strats at Playgroup level moving to PP1, PP2,Grades 1 to Grade 6 level respectively, our school recognizes the significance of laying a strong educational foundation. These early years embody a crucial stage in a student's educational journey in shaping the child's curiosity, social skills, and love for learning. Here, the foundation laid in the early years is fortified, and students are introduced to a more diverse and comprehensive curriculum. Our nurturing and stimulating environment focuses on holistic development, incorporating play-based learning, interactive activities, and a supportive atmosphere
